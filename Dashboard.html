Experience

During my time in junior high school, I actively participated in various extracurricular activities and organizations that helped shape my skills and character.

-Silat
I was deeply involved in silat, the traditional Indonesian martial art, and achieved the rank of green belt. My dedication and hard work in this discipline paid off as I successfully competed and won in several competitions, reaching the district level.

-GOJAKO Day(Gombong Japan Korea)
I was also an active member of the student organization, where I took on the role of fundraising coordinator. In this capacity, I organized and managed fundraising activities, successfully raising 7 million IDR for our school's event, GOJAKO-DAY. This experience taught me valuable lessons in leadership, teamwork, and financial management.

-Basketball
In addition to my involvement in silat and student organizations, I was a member of the school's basketball team. Participating in basketball helped me develop teamwork skills, discipline, and a strong sense of sportsmanship.

These experiences not only enriched my time in junior high school but also provided me with a solid foundation for my future endeavors.
