Sensasi

Sensasi is a scholarship program designed to support students with exceptional skills and achievements,enabling them to pursue a brighter future without financial constraints. The program collaborates with several major universities, including STTI Papua, STTI Yogya, UGM, ITB, Ukrim, and many more.

Objectives:

Scholarship Provision:
Offers scholarships to outstanding undergraduate students, particularly those from underdeveloped areas (3T regions).

Skill Development:
Equips students with both soft skills and academic hard skills.

Potential Enhancement:
Aims to explore, develop, and enhance students' potential through technological advancements.

Mission:

Build the competencies of the nation's future generations through education.

My Role:

I have the opportunity to serve as the IT pillar for Sensasi , where my responsibilities include maintaining the Sensasi website and developing both its frontend and backend components. This role allows me to leverage my technical skills to support a meaningful cause, contributing to the education and development of students from underdeveloped regions.
